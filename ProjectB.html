Welcome to the City of Chicago ~~

Chicago is famous for its city architecture, Last time I made a simple colorful model for Willis Tower, this time I made it possible for you to discover with a 3d camera that you can move around the space, please follow the instructions below, enjoy :) ~

Instructions:

use "W,A,S,D" to move around in the space

arrow keys to turn your head around

The RPM can be customized by user input, or click the buttons to increase/decrease

If the RPM >= 800, the holicopter will take off, and once RPM <= 800, it will start returning back to ground

Click "Reset Helicopter" to reset the position of the helicopter

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Please provide the RPM for helicopter (>= 800 to lift up)

Open the JavaScript Console, please:
(right-click-->'Inspect' or 'Inspect element'-->'Console' tab)
